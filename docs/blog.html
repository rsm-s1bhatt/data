Why You Shouldn't Peek at Your A/B Test

April 2026 · UCSD Marketing Analytics

A first-principles walkthrough of the classical frequentist toolkit through the lens of a simulated A/B test on newsletter sign-up CTAs. Covers the Law of Large Numbers, bootstrap vs. analytical standard errors, the Central Limit Theorem, hypothesis testing, the regression equivalence of the two-sample t-test, and a simulation showing how peeking at results early inflates the false positive rate well beyond the nominal 5%.
